Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2, 2026   |   Last Updated: March 2, 2026

BehindClimbing ("we," "our," or "the app") is developed and maintained as an independent iOS application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use BehindClimbing on your device. We take your privacy seriously and have designed this app with minimal data collection as a core principle.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or account credentials
  • Your precise or approximate location history
  • Device identifiers such as IDFA or IDFV
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking data
  • Payment or financial information
  • Health or fitness data

2. Location Data

BehindClimbing requests access to your device location solely to provide elevation-accurate air quality readings, nearby safe place results, and position-aware weather data. Location access is requested using the "When In Use" permission level, meaning the app only accesses your location while it is open and active in the foreground.

Your location is processed entirely on-device or transmitted directly to Apple's WeatherKit and MapKit services to retrieve weather and mapping data. We do not receive, log, or store your location coordinates on any server we operate.

3. WeatherKit and MapKit

BehindClimbing uses Apple WeatherKit to retrieve weather forecasts and Apple MapKit for mapping and reverse geocoding. When these services are used, your approximate location may be sent to Apple's servers to return relevant data. This data transfer is governed by Apple's own privacy policy, which can be reviewed at apple.com/legal/privacy.

We do not have access to the data exchanged between your device and Apple's services.

4. In-App Purchases

Subscription and purchase transactions are processed entirely through Apple's App Store payment system. We do not receive, handle, or store any payment card details or billing information. Purchase records are managed by Apple and are subject to Apple's terms and privacy policy.

5. Third-Party Services

BehindClimbing does not integrate any third-party analytics platforms, advertising SDKs, or social media frameworks. The app does not display advertisements of any kind.

6. Data Security

Because we do not collect or store personal data on external servers, there is no personal data at risk of a server-side breach. All app preferences and settings are stored locally on your device using Apple's standard iOS storage mechanisms.

7. Children's Privacy

BehindClimbing is intended for use by adults engaged in climbing and mountaineering activities. The app is not directed at children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has used this app and provided personal information in any way, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or applicable law. When changes are made, the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will be revised. Continued use of the app after a policy update constitutes your acceptance of the revised terms.
