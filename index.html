What's Inside

Every feature exists for one reason — the climb.

🌬️

Air Condition

Real-time AQI at base, mid, and summit layers. Wind direction, speed, and full pollutant breakdown including PM2.5, ozone, NO2, and SO2. Climber advisory tells you whether the air supports a push.

WeatherKit
⛰️

Mountain 3D

Three-dimensional peak overview with marked waypoints, route lines, and status indicators. Switch between terrain, snow depth, wind flow, and temperature zone layers. Tap any waypoint for live conditions.

MapKit
🏕️

Safe Zone

Find shelters, water sources, restrooms, rest areas, and mountain rescue posts near your position. Each shows distance, elevation, rating, and climber-specific tags. Navigate directly from the app.

Core Location
📡

Forecast Radar

Animated Doppler radar showing precipitation cells over 12 hours. Hourly summit conditions, storm cell ETA, and a calculated summit window with recommended ascent, summit, and descent times.
