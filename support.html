Accessing Your Plan Pages

There are two convenient ways to explore and select a plan.

Option 1 — From Go Premium

Step 1

Tap View Plans inside Go Premium.

Step 2

Choose from See Low Plan, See Moderate Plan, or See High Plan.

Step 3

You will be taken directly to the full details page for that tier.

Option 2 — From the Store

Step 1

Head to the Subscribe tab inside the Store.

Step 2

Tap Low, Moderate, or High.

Step 3

Choose from See Low Plan, See Moderate Plan, or See High Plan to view full details.

FAQ

Common Questions

Why does the app request my location?
BehindClimbing uses your location to provide elevation-accurate AQI readings, find nearby safe places such as shelters and water sources, and generate position-aware weather forecasts. Location is only accessed while the app is open and is never stored on any server we operate.
Does the app work offline?
Weather and radar data requires an active connection to retrieve from Apple WeatherKit. Safe place data and map tiles may be partially cached by the system. For climbing in areas with no connectivity, we recommend reviewing conditions before your approach.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Open the Settings app on your iPhone, tap your Apple ID at the top, go to Subscriptions, and select BehindClimbing. From there you can cancel or change your plan. Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period.
How accurate is the weather data?
Weather data is provided by Apple WeatherKit, which sources from professional meteorological networks. Accuracy varies by location and elevation. Mountain weather can change rapidly — always treat the app's forecast as one input among many, not as the sole basis for a go or no-go decision.
What does the AQI reading represent at altitude?
The Air Quality Index displayed in BehindClimbing reflects available air quality monitoring data interpolated for your current elevation. At very high altitudes, ground-level monitoring stations may be sparse. The reading is best used as a directional indicator rather than a precise measurement at extreme elevations.
Is BehindClimbing available on Android or Apple Watch?
BehindClimbing is currently available exclusively on iOS 26 and later. Android and watchOS versions are not available at this time.
